📚 常见问题

如何生成我的第一个命盘？

打开应用后，点击「新建命盘」，选择你的出生时代（古代/民国/现代），输入出生年月日时和性别，即可生成专属命盘。

命盘数据会保存在哪里？

所有命盘数据都保存在本地设备上，不会上传到任何服务器，确保你的隐私安全。

如何导出或分享命盘？

在命盘详情页面，点击右上角的分享按钮，可以将命盘导出为图片或文本格式保存到相册或文件。

支持哪些平台？

目前支持 iOS、macOS 和 Android 平台。Windows 版本正在开发中。

📚 Frequently Asked Questions

How do I generate my first birth chart?

Open the app and tap "New Chart". Select your birth era (Ancient/Republic/Modern), enter your birth date, time, and gender to generate your personalized chart.

Where is my chart data stored?

All chart data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server, ensuring your privacy.

How can I export or share my chart?

In the chart details page, tap the share button in the top right corner to export your chart as an image or text to your album or files.

Which platforms are supported?

Currently supports iOS, macOS, and Android platforms. Windows version is under development.

What if the app crashes or lags?

Please try restarting the app. If the issue persists, contact us using the methods below and we'll fix it ASAP.
